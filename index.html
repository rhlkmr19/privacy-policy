Information Collection and Use

White Board does not collect any personally identifiable information such as name, address, or phone number.

Any drawings, boards, or images created by the user are stored locally on the user's device only and are never uploaded to any server.

Drawing Data & Local Storage

The app allows users to:

  • Draw and write on a digital whiteboard
  • Save drawings in app history (locally)
  • Export drawings to the device gallery
  • Share drawing images via other apps

All saved content remains on the user's device. We do not access, transmit, or store this data externally.

Offline Usage

White Board works completely offline. An internet connection is not required for drawing, saving, or viewing previously created boards.

Advertisements (AdMob)

AdMob may collect certain non-personal information such as:

This information is used solely for serving relevant ads. AiTricker does not have access to this data.
